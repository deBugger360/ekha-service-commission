Chairman's Goodwill message

Welcome to Ekiti House of Assembly Service Commission

I, Hon (Eng) Kayode Fasakin, the Executive Chairman, Ekiti State House of Assembly Service Commission on behalf of Commissioners, Staff and Management of the Commission welcome all stakeholders and the general public to this website. This website is an platform that enables the general public to have access to information about activities concerning the Commission anywhere and anytime, real time. As you are aware, the world is now a global village, through here, interested members of the public can access information on daily happenings as related to the personnel in the Ekiti State Legislature. We are thrilled to share this new presence with you. Our vision is to offer effective management of personnel and provide support services in the legislative arm of government. Many thanks to all that make the dream a reality. Together, let's strive for excellence in legislative service. You are welcome here. We hope you will always enjoy the interface.
